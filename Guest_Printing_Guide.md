# Print Documents (Guest Users)

## Overview
This guide explains how non-affiliated users can print documents using public workstations and printers in the university library.

---

## Print a Document

1. Log in to a public workstation:
   - Username: `bgsu1969`  
   - Password: `bgsu1969`  

2. Open your document and select **Print**

3. In the print dialog:
   - Confirm the printer is **BG-Print-LPR**
   - Select **Print**

4. When prompted, enter your credentials:
   - Username: guest card number  
   - Password: `guest`  
   - Select **OK**

5. Confirm the print job was submitted:
   - Select **OK** when the confirmation window appears  

6. Go to any public printer/copier and swipe your guest card

7. Release your print job:
   - Select **Print Release**  
   - Choose your document(s)  
   - Select **Print**

8. When finished:
   - Select **Log Out** on the printer interface

## Pricing

- Black & white: $0.05 per page  
- Color: $0.20 per page  

To check your balance:
- Select **Account Selection** from the printer home screen

## Troubleshooting

### Print job not appearing
- Confirm the correct printer (**BG-Print-LPR**) was selected  
- Ensure the correct guest card number was entered  

### Unable to log in
- Verify username and password are both entered correctly  
- Restart the workstation if needed  

---

## Notes
- Always log out after completing your session  
- Keep your guest card available for printing and authentication

# Managing Item Checkouts in Alma

## Overview
This guide explains how to check out library materials using the Alma library management system. It covers standard workflows, special cases, and renewal guidelines.

---

## Check Out Items to a Patron

### Requirements

- Only current students, faculty, staff, and courtesy card holders may check out items  
- Verify patron identity before proceeding:
  - Preferred: **BGSU ID card**  
  - Alternative: official photo ID (driver’s license, passport, etc.)  
  - If unavailable: verify using the patron’s **Canvas dashboard**  

- Outstanding fines or overdue items do not prevent checkout for eligible users  

---

### Standard Checkouts (Jerome Library and OhioLINK)

1. Open the patron search:
   - Press `Alt + W` to access **Patron Identification**

2. Locate the patron:
   - Scan their ID card  
   - Or search by name and select the correct record  

3. Open the patron account:
   - Select **Go**

4. Scan item barcodes:
   - Each item will be added to the account  

5. Desensitize items:
   - Pass items over the desensitizer  

6. Confirm checkout:
   - Inform the patron of the due date  

---

### Interlibrary Loan (ILL) Checkouts

1. Open the patron account as in a standard checkout  

2. Note:
   - ILL items do not appear in the patron’s Alma account  

3. Inform the patron of the due date:
   - Use the date listed on the item slip  

4. Desensitize the item  

5. Complete processing:
   - Write initials and date on the slip  
   - Place the slip in the designated basket  

**Library-use-only items:**
- Identified by a yellow flag  
- If returned unfinished, place back on the hold shelf  

---

### Laptop Checkouts

Laptops have specific requirements and handling procedures.

**Key rules:**
- Loan period: **7 days**  
- A charger must be issued with each laptop  
- A **Laptop Agreement** must be completed  

#### Checkout Process

1. Check for an existing Laptop Agreement in the patron record  

2. If none exists:
   - Have the patron complete and sign the agreement  
   - Complete the staff section of the form  

3. Submit the form to the Desk Supervisor  

4. Provide usage instructions if needed:
   - Initial login must occur on campus  

5. Issue the laptop and charger  

6. Do not desensitize the laptop  

#### Additional Notes

- Laptops may be renewed up to system limits if no holds exist  
- Laptops must be returned to the service desk  
- Do not check in a laptop without the charger  
- If no laptops are available, place a hold for the patron  

---

## Renewal Guidelines

Patrons may renew items through multiple channels:
- Online via their account  
- In person  
- By phone, email, or chat  

Always verify patron identity before discussing account details.

---

### Common Renewal Issues

Items may not renew for the following reasons:

- **Maximum renewals reached**
  - Items may be checked in and reissued if no holds exist  

- **Active hold by another patron**
  - Item must be returned  

- **Overdue status**
  - Renewal must be completed within Alma  

- **Non-renewable item type**
  - Includes reserves, equipment, and select devices  

---

## Notes

- Follow system prompts for holds and restrictions  
- Escalate unresolved issues to a supervisor
